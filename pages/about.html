unified family services - our story

Unified Stance Family Services LLC. is a Mental Health / Wellness consulting company that promotes Positive Mental health, Emotional literacy, Social emotional Learning and positive identity formation. Helping children and adults develop strong self-concepts through Social Emotional learning and trauma awareness. We specifically employ proven evidence-based education, resources, training and engaging activities with the purpose of instilling healthy pathways/coping mechanisms to Emotional wellness.

Unified Stance Family services was founded by Hanif Akinyemi. Hanif is a Classically trained mental health consultant. He received a bachelor’s in nursing from Florida A&M University. He then spent 8 years in Mental Health specializing in trauma awareness and social emotional learning. He then went on to get his master’s in social work and began his Work as a Social Emotional Learning Specialist and Trauma Awareness advocate. He himself has had to overcome many obstacles on the road emotional wellness. He went from an anxious, depressed, people pleaser to living life on his on his own terms learning the skills necessary to living a fulfilling emotionally healthy life! He suffered from not knowing how to communicate or express himself in a healthy way. Through his own educational training as a mental health consultant, coaching and difficult life experiences he changed his life forever strengthening his emotional and mental self. Now he wants do the same for the community by educating, coaching and promoting emotional wellness.


our mission

Success for tomorrow through knowledge and understanding obtained Today!

We aim to transform lives through education, health and personal development based on the four pillars of human essence: mental, emotional, physical and spiritual. Our objectives are to promote social inclusion, education for life and foster health and well-being.
